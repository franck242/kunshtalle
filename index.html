CURRENT EXIBITION

10/06/2022 au 30/10/2022.

xhumer le futur - Maarten Vanden Eynde

Maarten Vanden Eynde's work questions the close links that exist
the model of economic growth and the decline of the Earth.
The exhibition Exhumer le Futur, and the catalog of the same name that
accompanies it, retraces his artistic practice from 2000 to 2020, a period
marked by numerous technological advances but also by successive crises.
Exhibition curator: Katerina Gregos and Sandrine Wymann.
